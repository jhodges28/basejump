BASEJUMP

The Joomla Base Theme for Web Artisans

Introducing BaseJump; a starter theme built for Joomla and designed from the ground up to stay the heck out of your way

As awesome as the most popular Joomla starter themes are, their bloated, overcompensating stylesheets can be frustrating to work with. My own development was marred with wasted time "de-styling" my websites. There had to be a better way. As the existing minimal templates proved to be too limited, BaseJump was born.

BaseJump is built with just enough components and styling to make your job easier, leaving you with room to create your own amazing features. So leave your !important tags at home and get ready for some lean Joomla development.
